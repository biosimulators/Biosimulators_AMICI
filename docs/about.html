About BioSimulators-AMICI¶

License¶

This package is released under the MIT License. AMICI is released under the 3-Clause BSD License.

Development team¶

This package was developed by the Karr Lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and the Center for Reproducible Biomedical Modeling. AMICI was developed by the Hasenauer Lab at the Limes-Institut.

Questions and comments¶

Please contact the BioSimulators Team with any questions or comments about the command-line program or Docker image. Please contact the AMICI Team with any questions or comments about AMICI.
